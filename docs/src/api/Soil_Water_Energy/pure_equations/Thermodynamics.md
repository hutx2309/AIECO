# Thermodynamics

Source: [`src/Soil_Water_Energy/pure_equations/Thermodynamics.jl`](https://github.com/hutx2309/AIECO/blob/main/src/Soil_Water_Energy/pure_equations/Thermodynamics.jl)

Pure helpers for saturation vapor concentration, vapor equilibrium under water
potential, latent and advective heat, material heat capacity, temperature from
energy, equilibrium temperature, and conductive/sensible heat exchange.

These functions return values and do not mutate model state.
